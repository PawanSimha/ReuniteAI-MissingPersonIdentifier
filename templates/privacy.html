Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2026

Website: ReuniteAI

1. Data Collected

1.1 Analytics (Vercel Web Analytics)

We use Vercel Web Analytics, a privacy-first tool that collects:

  • Page URL and referrer
  • Browser type and device category
  • Country-level geographic data (no IP storage)

No cookies, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking. Data is aggregated and anonymized.

1.2 Contact Form

If you submit the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message content

This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is never sold or shared.

1.3 Uploaded Images

Images uploaded for missing person identification are stored temporarily for matching and then deleted. Facial encodings are extracted and stored as numerical vectors only — original images are not retained.

2. Legal Basis (GDPR)

  • Analytics: Legitimate interest (website improvement)
  • Contact form: Consent (you explicitly submit it)
  • Image processing: Public interest (reuniting missing persons)

3. Data Retention

  • Analytics data: 12 months (Vercel auto-rotation)
  • Contact form submissions: Deleted within 90 days after resolution
  • Uploaded images: Deleted immediately after encoding extraction
  • Face encodings: Retained until the missing person is found or the record is removed

4. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing pawansimha@gmail.com.
