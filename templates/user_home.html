Process Workflow

How ReuniteAI Works

Our secure, privacy-preserving identification pipeline explained step-by-step, for user convinence and for user understadability

cloud_upload

01. Secure Upload

User uploads an image. The file is encrypted in transit and processed in volatile memory, ensuring data privacy.

face

02. Feature Extraction

The AI model detects the face and generates a 128-dimensional vector embedding representing unique facial landmarks.

compare_arrows

03. Vector Matching

The system computes Euclidean distances between the uploaded vector and stored embeddings to find potential matches.

verified

04. Verification

Matches above the confidence threshold are presented. Users can then view details or register a new case if no match is found.

Why ReuniteAI Matters

Every year, thousands of families face the anguish of separation. Missing persons cases often go cold due to limited resources, jurisdictional barriers, and the sheer scale of manual identification efforts.

ReuniteAI bridges this gap by democratizing access to advanced facial recognition technology. We empower families, NGOs, and communities with tools that were once available only to well-funded institutions.

Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Is uploaded data safe?
Yes. All data is encrypted both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We use industry-standard security protocols to ensure that sensitive information remains private and is only used for matching purposes. Raw images are never stored in our encrypted embeddings.
Who can access the photos?
Only authorized researchers and system administrators with multi-factor authentication have access to the encrypted data. The public cannot browse the database; they can only perform specific verification searches against their own uploaded images.
What if no match is found?
If no match is found immediately, the data remains securely stored (with your explicit consent) to be cross-referenced with future reports. You can request deletion of your data at any time through your account settings.
Is this linked to police databases?
No. ReuniteAI is an independent academic research project. We do not have direct integration with law enforcement databases at this stage. However, we are open to ethical partnerships that respect privacy and consent.
Is this free to use?
Yes, ReuniteAI is completely free. It is a social good initiative developed for academic research and community support. We have no commercial interests and will never charge for basic identification services.
Can NGOs use this platform?
Absolutely. We welcome collaboration with NGOs and humanitarian organizations. Please contact us at research@reuniteai.edu for partnership opportunities, including bulk reporting features and integration support.
How accurate is the facial recognition?
In controlled laboratory settings, our system achieves 98% accuracy. However, real-world performance depends on image quality, lighting, aging, and other factors. All matches undergo manual review before contact information is released.
What about bias in facial recognition?
We acknowledge that facial recognition systems can exhibit bias. Our models are trained on diverse, internationally representative datasets, and we continuously evaluate performance across demographic groups. Bias mitigation is an active area of our research.
